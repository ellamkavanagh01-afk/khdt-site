Kilkenny’s Dark Past

From witch trials to whispered apparitions, Kilkenny’s lanes hold centuries of shadows. Our tour follows the city’s oldest passages where echoes linger, guiding you by warm lantern light.

Expect true stories, local lore and a gothic atmosphere — not jump scares. Suitable for most ages.

FAQ

Where do we meet?

Outside the Butter Slip arch off High Street, Kilkenny. Look for the lantern.

Is it suitable for children?

Yes — this is a storytelling walk. We keep it eerie, not gory.

What should I bring?

Comfortable shoes, weather-appropriate clothing and a sense of adventure.
