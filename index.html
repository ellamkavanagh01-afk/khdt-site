Step into Kilkenny’s dark past

Step into the shadows of Kilkenny’s medieval streets and uncover the city’s darkest secrets. Our evening walking tour brings you face to face with the real haunted history of Ireland’s most atmospheric city.

Beginning at the magnificent Kilkenny Castle, you’ll journey through centuries of chilling tales — from ghostly apparitions to eerie legends whispered through time. Follow your guide down Mary’s Lane and through the narrow Butter Slip, where spirits are said to linger long after nightfall. The tour concludes at the historic Courthouse, a site steeped in stories of trials, tragedy, and restless souls.

Every story told on this tour is rooted in real events, real people, and real hauntings — no gimmicks, no jump scares, just the true dark side of Kilkenny brought to life through expert storytelling.

Join us if you dare... and see Kilkenny as you’ve never seen it before.

FAQs

Is it suitable for children?

Our standard walks focus on history and folklore with a spooky atmosphere rather than jump scares. We can adjust tone for younger groups with advance notice.

What should I bring?

Comfortable shoes, a warm layer and a small umbrella if rain is forecast. The route is mostly flat with regular stops.

Is it accessible?

Parts of the old town have cobbles and narrow paths. Let us know any requirements and we will plan the most suitable route.

What happens in bad weather?

Tours run in most conditions. In severe weather we will contact you with options to reschedule or refund.
