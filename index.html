Our standard walks focus on history and folklore with a spooky atmosphere rather than jump scares. We can adjust tone for younger groups with advance notice.

Comfortable shoes, a warm layer and a small umbrella if rain is forecast. The route is mostly flat with regular stops.

Parts of the old town have cobbles and narrow paths. Let us know any requirements and we will plan the most suitable route.

Tours run in most conditions. In severe weather we will contact you with options to reschedule or refund.
